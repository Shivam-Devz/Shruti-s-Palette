About Me

Hi, I'm Shruti Jaiswal, a passionate visual artist from Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, exploring the world through ink, oil, and watercolor. My work is where silence finds color and emotions find form.

I discovered my love for painting as a child, captivated by how colors could express what words sometimes cannot. Over the years, I honed my skills through art classes at:

  • Vikas School of Arts, Estd 1978
    Affiliated to:
    Bangiya Sangeet Parishad under West Bengal Govt., Registration Act C/2931.JH of 1992
    &
    Bharati Kala Kendra, Lucknow Under UP Govt., Registration Act E/JH/J/180/224 of 2004

I work primarily with watercolor, ink, and oil, often blending delicate strokes with bold textures. My art is inspired by nature, emotions, and fleeting moments that linger in memory.

Throughout my journey, I have earned certificates and recognition for my work, which motivate me to continue exploring and expressing through art.

I believe that art should evoke emotion and spark reflection. Each painting is a moment captured in time, a whisper of thought or feeling translated into color and form.

I invite you to explore my gallery and share in the stories that each painting tells. Thank you for visiting Shruti's Palette!
